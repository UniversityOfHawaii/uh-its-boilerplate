Primary Typeface: Open Sans

Open Sans,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif

The primary typeface is Open Sans, a humanist sans serif typeface available on Google Fonts. We use Regular, Semi-Bold, and Bold weights along with their italic styles. We do not use light weight because it is less legible on low-resolution screens. We use Open Sans for both the body and display text.
